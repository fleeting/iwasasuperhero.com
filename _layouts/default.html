I'm James Fleeting, a web designer and developer in Wichita Falls, Texas. I specialize in front-end development with a passion for HTML and CSS. My obsession for the future of the web pushes me to never stop learning and this blog is where I share that information. You can also find me on twitter where I post mostly what I'm eating or listening to. When I'm not working, I can be found playing video games, watching movies or spending time with my wife and kids.
