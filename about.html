Current Work

I’m not sure what others call me, but I call myself a web designer and developer. I first start dabbling in web design back in 2001 and have been obsessed with it since. I really started doing web development professionaly in 2003. I was a freelance web designer for 5 years doing a wide range of client work. I worked with WordPress exclusively during that time. August 2008 began a new phase for me as I started working for DigiMedia.com in Wichita Falls as a web designer and Ruby on Rails developer. I was part of a small team developing a large scale e-commerce solution to power hundreds of domains. After a year at DigiMedia I was given a great opportunity and left the company to work for CRANE | WEST in October 2009. I’m currently the senior front-end developer and have worked on a variety of client websites and projects along with developing our custom in-house Content Management System. Beyond typical websites I have done Facebook customizations, been a part of social campaigns, planned and filmed television commercials, developed internal web apps and help manage two digital billboards.
